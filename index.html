About Poly

Paulette Maroun Matta, known by her stage name Poly, is a Lebanese artist born in Mexico. She graduated from the Institute of Contemporary Music Performance (ICMP) in London at just 16, and trained with industry names like Vic Bynoe and Me’sha Bryan.

Career Highlights

  • 🎶 Genre: Soul / Pop
  • 📺 Appeared in a Christmas Medley (2010) and MTV’s Hek Menghanne with Maya Diab (2011)
  • 🎤 Performed originals like Tough and Eating For Two at DRM concerts
  • 🎶 Participated in Beirut’s Fête de la Musique
  • 🌈 Advocate for LGBT rights and freedom of expression

Style & Persona

Poly is known for her bold and provocative image. She often designs outfits from unconventional or recycled materials like newspapers, cardboard, and bubble wrap, making her stand out as a fashion icon as much as a musician.
